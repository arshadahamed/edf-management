EDF GALGAMUWA
Our Mission

Empowering
communities,
one family
at a time.

EDF Galgamuwa was founded on a single conviction: every child deserves an education and every family deserves a life of dignity. We address root causes of poverty through sustainable, community-led programs.

Explore Programs
🎓

Education First

Scholarships, tutoring centres, and school supplies for children from low-income families. We ensure no child is left without learning.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Free mobile clinics, mental health support, and medicine subsidies for families who cannot afford basic medical care.

💼

Livelihood & Jobs

Vocational training, micro-financing, and job linkages to break cycles of poverty and build lasting financial independence.

🌙

Ramadan & Zakat Aid

Annual Ramadan food packs, Eid grants, and Zakat distributions — ensuring no family goes without during sacred seasons.

Program 03
03
💼

Livelihood & Youth Development

We offer free vocational training programs such as mobile phone repair, CCTV installation, and entrepreneurship development. Our initiatives also include business and tax guidance, educational bayans, and one-day skill workshops. In addition, we provide financial assistance to selected university students in Galgamuwa to support their living expenses and continue their education without hardship.
