C — Dual Mode (View / Manage tabs)

Modal has two tabs: View History (current read-only experience with search/filter/export) and Manage Payments (CRUD-only, no clutter). Clean separation of concerns.

Pros

  • Read-only view stays clean and uncluttered
  • Clear intent: "I'm here to manage"

Cons

  • Extra click to switch modes
  • Duplicates some rendering logic
